The Solution — SAARTHI

Secure. Fair. Transparent.

SAARTHI is an AI-assisted verification platform that validates eligibility for benefits under schemes like PM-AJAY and GIA through layered checks, consent, and explainability.

  • Privacy-by-design: consent flows, minimal data retention.
  • Explainable decisions: clear reasons, appeal pathways.
  • Offline-first: works in low connectivity with auto-sync.

Technical Flow

How SAARTHI Works

Layered verification + three-channel architecture for fairness and trust.

Layer 01

Identity & Community

Basic identity and community data — Aadhaar, caste certificate verification with explicit user consent.

Fast KYC linkage via DigiLocker
Layer 02

Income & Lifestyle Indicators

Signals from government DBT records, ration card activity, education data — without permanently storing sensitive data.

API-driven, consented checks
Layer 03

AI Eligibility Scoring

We analyze spending patterns, area development index, and household data to predict real economic condition and assign a trust score.

Fairness example: A rural student earning ₹6000/month without updated income records still gets recognized through digital footprints and community data.
Layer 04

Transparency & Audit

Immutable audit logs and verifiable decision trails ensure zero manipulation. Every decision is explainable and accessible to the applicant.

Signed logs + role-based access
Layer 05

Benefit Disbursal & Feedback

Auto-notifications on approval, direct benefit transfer tracking, and grievance redressal with time-bound SLAs.

Citizen-first: status in local language, offline SMS fallback, and helpline integration.
